ORDER MANAGEMENT

How to Track Your Order:
1. Log into your Genesis E-commerce account
2. Navigate to "My Orders" section
3. Click on the specific order number
4. View real-time tracking information
5. You can also use our API endpoint: GET /v1/orders/user/{user_id}

Order Status Meanings:
- PENDING: Order received and awaiting processing
- PROCESSING: Order is being prepared for shipment
- SHIPPED: Order has been dispatched and is in transit
- DELIVERED: Order has been successfully delivered
- CANCELLED: Order has been cancelled
- RETURNED: Order has been returned

How to Modify an Order:
- Orders can only be modified within 2 hours of placement
- Contact customer support immediately with your order number
- Provide details of the changes you need
- Modifications are subject to product availability

RETURNS AND REFUNDS

Return Eligibility:
- Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
- Products must be unused and in original packaging
- Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation
- Retain proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

Non-Returnable Items:
- Digital products and downloadable software
- Personalized or customized items
- Perishable goods
- Intimate or sanitary products
- Gift cards

Return Process:
1. Contact customer support to initiate a return
2. Provide your order number and reason for return
3. Receive a Return Authorization (RA) number
4. Pack the item securely with the RA number
5. Ship to the provided return address
6. Track your return shipment
7. Refund processed within 7-10 business days after receipt

Refund Methods:
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Processing time: 7-10 business days
- Bank processing may take additional 3-5 business days
- You will receive an email confirmation when refund is processed

SHIPPING INFORMATION

Shipping Options:
1. Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): $5.99
2. Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $14.99
3. Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $24.99
4. Free Standard Shipping on orders over $50

International Shipping:
- Available to select countries
- Customs fees and import duties are customer's responsibility
- Delivery times vary by destination (10-21 business days)
- International orders cannot be expedited

Shipping Restrictions:
- We do not ship to PO Boxes for express delivery
- Some products cannot be shipped internationally
- Address changes must be made before shipment
- Incorrect addresses may result in delivery delays

Lost or Damaged Shipments:
- Report lost packages within 30 days of expected delivery
- Report damaged items within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of damaged packaging and products
- We will investigate and provide replacement or refund

PAYMENT ISSUES

Accepted Payment Methods:
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Genesis E-commerce Gift Cards

Payment Declined:
Common reasons and solutions:
1. Insufficient funds - Check your account balance
2. Incorrect card details - Verify card number, CVV, and expiration
3. Billing address mismatch - Ensure address matches card records
4. Security hold - Contact your bank
5. Card expired - Use a valid payment method

Billing Questions:
- Charges appear as "Genesis E-commerce" on statements
- You will receive a receipt via email after purchase
- For duplicate charges, contact support immediately
- Promotional codes must be applied before payment

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Password Reset:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check email for reset link (valid for 24 hours)
4. Create a new strong password
5. Passwords must be at least 8 characters with mixed case and numbers

Update Account Information:
- Log into your account
- Navigate to "Account Settings"
- Update email, phone, or shipping address
- Save changes
- Verify email if email address is changed

Account Security:
- Never share your password with anyone
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
- Review account activity regularly
- Log out from shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

SUPPORT TICKET SYSTEM

Creating a Support Ticket:
1. Use the API endpoint: POST /v1/support/tickets
2. Provide your order_id and detailed issue_description
3. You'll receive a ticket ID for tracking
4. Our team will respond within 24 hours

Ticket Priority Levels:
- LOW: General inquiries, non-urgent questions
- MEDIUM: Order issues, shipping questions
- HIGH: Payment problems, urgent order changes
- CRITICAL: Account security, unauthorized transactions

Checking Ticket Status:
- Use API endpoint: GET /v1/support/tickets/{ticket_id}
- Status values: OPEN, IN_PROGRESS, RESOLVED, CLOSED
- You'll receive email updates on ticket progress

Best Practices for Support Tickets:
- Include your order number
- Provide detailed description of the issue
- Attach relevant screenshots or photos
- Specify your preferred resolution
- Respond promptly to support team inquiries

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, express takes 2-3 business days. International orders take 10-21 business days.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
A: Only if the order hasn't shipped yet. Contact support immediately with your order number.

Q: What if I receive the wrong item?
A: Contact support within 48 hours with photos. We'll arrange for correct item to be sent and provide a prepaid return label.

Q: Are there any hidden fees?
A: No. The total at checkout is your final price (excluding international customs fees which vary by country).

Q: How do I cancel an order?
A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours if not yet shipped. Contact support with your order number.

Q: Can I track my return?
A: Yes, keep your return shipping receipt and tracking number. Check with the carrier for updates.

Q: What if my promo code doesn't work?
A: Check expiration date, minimum purchase requirements, and that eligible items are in your cart. Contact support if issues persist.

Q: How do I contact a live agent?
A: Create a support ticket via our API or use the chatbot. For urgent matters, mark ticket as HIGH priority.
